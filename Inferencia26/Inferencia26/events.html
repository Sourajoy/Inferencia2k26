Professor Mrinal Kanti Basu Memorial Talk

Professor Mrinal Kanti Basu

Professor Mrinal Kanti Basu, a revered statistician and educator known for his teaching at the Ramakrishna Mission Residential College (Autonomous). After enrolling in 1976 in this college he overcame a year-long battle with stomach ulcers to graduate in 1980 and complete his Master’s in 1983. A superb teacher of mathematical statistics was a double gold medalist who consistently topped his examinations. Though health issues prevented him from attending the Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), he became a celebrated educator known for simplifying complex topics like Linear Models. Beyond the classroom, his sharp intellect made him a nearly unbeatable chess player. His passing in 2003 due to Non-hodgkin's Lymphoma left a profound void in the academic community.
